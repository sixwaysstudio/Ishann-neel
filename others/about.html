Behind the lens, chasing light and shadows.

My Journey

I always wondered if I could show other people how I viewed the world. That made me start my journey with my mom's Nokia 6300 and since then I've been a vagabond walking through the world creating my own shutterstory.


Exhibition and Judging

Throughout this journey, two of my photos got exhibited at Catch The Light, season 3 organised by y CUET Photographic Society. I have also served as a judge for Campus Photography Contest 2025 organized by CU Statistics 54.


It's still a small feat but I'm learning everyday to reach new heights.
